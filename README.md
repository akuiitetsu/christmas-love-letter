## How to Add Your Photos

Replace the placeholder image files with your own photos:

1. **image1.jpg** - Your first photo (top left)
2. **image2.jpg** - Your second photo (top right)
3. **image3.jpg** - Your third photo (bottom left)
4. **image4.jpg** - Your fourth photo (bottom right)

Place these 4 images in the same folder as `index.html`.

## Photo Requirements

- Format: JPG, JPEG, or PNG
- Recommended size: 400x500 pixels (or similar portrait orientation)
- The photos will be cropped to fit the frames

## Customization

To personalize the letter:

1. Open `index.html`
2. Find the line with `Your [Your Name]`
3. Replace `[Your Name]` with your actual name
